Overview and Rates

Overview

Our Motorhome park was opened 12 months ago and in that short space of time has won numerous awards from tourism and International motorhome bodies. We are famous for our warm welcome and will help in any possible to make your stay as enjoyable as possible. The motorhome park is situated in a beautiful part of the Wild Atlantic mid way between Lahinch and Spanish Point. Our office opening hours are between 8am and 6pm daily, for earlier or later arrivals please call and special arrangements can be faciltated.
